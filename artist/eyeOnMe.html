Artist Statement 創作自述:

This is an Experiment about how will people behave in front of the camera.

In the very beginning of camera come to China of the late Qing Dynasty, people thought that their soul will be captured away threw the lens. Today, when people discover a shooting camera, they usually eyes on lens at the 1st moment, then escape away very quickly as if they still feel fear of being spirit away.

If lens really capturing people's soul. How much soul will be captured on the high population street within the period of a song? Come on people;don't turn your back on me!
