Artist Statement 創作自述:

This is my personal a fundraising campaign for supporting The Five Constituencies Referendum Movement 2010.
Also, simlar to the work "Tank Roll Over Piggies" , I was trying to do a counter-propaganda to the DAB in the internet 2.0 way.

這是一個由我個人發動的籌款運動，目的是協助2010年的五區公投運動。
另外，就像《坦克碌豬 快.狠.準 》一樣，我嘗試替親中共政團民建聯作internet 2.0式反宣傳。
