Artist Statement 創作自述:

'One country two system' is just a make up. Our election is actually more and more under Beijing's control by the unfair rules, both written rules and hidden rules.

If a game is not fair by the setup of rules, what's the point to keep playing it "courteously"and keep on losing for 10 years? It's time to change!

「如果一盤棋，對方每局可以行兩次，又可以飛象過河，你仲同佢正正經經咁捉，玩足十年輸足十年，(按：然後又係咁"盡左力無計啦")，咁，你係傻既!」蕭若元評香港民主運動
