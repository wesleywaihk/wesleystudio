Artist Statement 創作自述:

"Have a Gay Pic" is a project jointly created by PrideLab and Wesley Studio, who are hoping to express the flight for equality in a cultural and artistic way. In this event, we created an Android app which allows users to put stamps and declarations onto the photos.

Users take photos, adding stamps, then upload their final work to the activity's Facebook Fanpage, or save to their phones for publication in other social websites.

We hope to save up over 1,000 photo supporting LGBT rights! No matter who you are, please take part in this event and show your love and support!

《Have a Gay Pic》 是創作團體PrideLab聯同Wesley Studio舉辦之活動，希望透過創作藝術以文化抗爭，爭取平權。 是次活動透過照相應用程式，讓用戶在相片中加入原創的圖案及宣言，以支持同志的意念。 用戶可以拍攝相片，加入自選圖案後上傳相片至活動專頁。用戶亦能儲存相片，並分享至其他社交網站。

我們希望收集超過一千張支持同志平等相片！無論你是同志還是直人，都希望今年你透過活動，用一張照片撐同志。
