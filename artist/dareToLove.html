11.10 Dare to Love: Gay Parade Hong Kong 2012 / 2012

Direct Link: http://wesley.com.hk/artist/dareToLove.html

Artist Statement 創作自述:

This series is photographed on 10 November 2012 during The Gay Parade HK. It is the continuum of '7.29 No Brain Washing Education' and other works of social movement. However its expression direction follows the previous installation work'I'm I ?'

In the journey of life, we are sometimes abstained from expressing ourselves truly. Often, homosexuals have to hide themselves from the majority. Hence, they were invited to kiss in front of the camera, showing their true colours through their outfits, accessories and action.

Surprisingly, some of those photographed kissers told us they are not actually gays or lesbians. Moreover, I discovered later on in this series a cross-dressed guy kissing a girl. It let me think of an old chinese poem:

"The male rabbit is swifter of foot, The eyes of the female are somewhat smaller.But when the two rabbits run side by side, How can you tell the female from the male?" [Ballad of Mulan]

 

這一輯相集攝於2012年11月10日同志遊行中，是"7.29保護腦袋"及其他社運主題作品的延續，而表達的主題卻繼承自裝置作品"我是我?"。

人生在世，總會被外在因素局限了自我的表現而不能忠實地表現自己。同志就是一個常常要把自我躲在衣櫃內的社群。如是者，我邀請他們在鏡頭前親吻，用他們的遊行道具，衣飾以及最直接的行動表現自我。

有趣的是，當中有朋友事後告訴我其實他們不是同志，也有後期製作時才發現一個crossdress男生和一個女生接吻，可謂雄兔腳撲朔，雌兔眼迷離。
