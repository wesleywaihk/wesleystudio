Artist Statement 創作自述:

As China artist Ai Wei Wei was captured by the China government in April 2011, Hong Kong Arts Discovery Channel (HKADC) has form a compaign for asking for releasing Ai Wei Wei and call for public entry.

For a quick response to the call, we set up a booth in the Mongkok and invite people to take an image that associating Ai Wei Wei's work. By this action, we express our desire to freedom, human rights, justice and the release of Ai Wei Wei.

因中國藝術家艾未未於四月被中國政府俱禁，香港藝術搜索頻道發起"徵集圖像：《呼籲立即釋放艾未未及國內其他以言入罪的維權人士》"(後來改名為《放手，我們都是艾未未》)並徵求公眾參與。

為迅速響應號召，我們於旺角行人專用區設立攤檔，呼籲途人拍下跟艾未未作品《掉漢瓶》關聯的影像。透過是次活動，我們表達了我們對自由人權和正義的渴求。
