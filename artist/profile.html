Artist Profile

Wesley Studio – Artist Section
Brief Proflie 簡歷:

WAI Ming-Sum Wesley, graduate of RMIT/Hong Kong Art School, majoring in Fine Art Photography. He is also graduated from Hong Kong Institute of Vocational Education (currently Hong Kong Design Institute) for a Visual Communication Design programme.

Since year 2000, Wesley is engaged in multamultimedia design and visual interface design works for over 10 years.

As an artist, he is interested in such topics as politics, living tensions and insecurities.

王小四（取名自本名衞銘琛的琛字)，畢業於皇家墨爾本理工大學/香港藝術學院，於純藝術學系主修攝影。此外他亦曾於香港專業教育學院（現在的香港知專設計學院）修畢視覺傳達設計課程。

自2000年起，小四從事多媒體設計及操作介面設計工作已超過十年。

作為藝術工作者，小四對民生政治及生活壓力與不安全感得題才尤感興趣。
