The Mini Nature I 迷理生態 I / 2008 / Digital Media 數碼媒體
Direct Link: http://wesley.com.hk/artist/miniNature.html

 

Instructions 說明:
  • There are 4 creatures in this mini ecosystem,each of them have a different strength on action and reproduction
    在這個迷你生態圈中有四種生物，牠們各自有不同的行動力和繁殖能力。

  • Each of them restraining another and formed an food chain.
    四種物種互相克制，形成一條食物鍊。

  • The 4 creatures on the right is the figure of the food chain, when one of the tribal become extinct, the related creature will disappear.
    右面四隻生物是食物鍊結構圖， 當其中一種生物滅絕，其右方的代表亦會消失

  • When one tribal become extinct, the food train breaks, other tribals will also dissappear 1 by 1, therefore the world ends.
    當其中一種物種無以為繼，食物鏈斷裂，其他物種亦會相繼滅亡，即世界毀滅了。

  • In theory loss(or called errors/entrophies) will always accumulate in any close system, therefore the food chain will always break finally.
    基於任何封閉系統的運作過程中都必然會累績損耗(或稱為熵/混亂)的理論，食物鍊最後必然會斷裂。

  • Visitors can act as god, press "The General Judgement" button, end the world of this mini nature.
    觀眾可以扮演天神，按下"大審判(The General Judgement)"按鈕，直接把迷理生態的世界毀滅。

  • Many factors that out of the system may also end the world, such as computer hang or user switch of the computer etc.
    此外，世界外的因素影響，例如電腦當機或用者關機等，世界也會毀滅。

  • In other words, there must be an end to the Mini Nature world, and so do our world.
    換言之，對世界中的生物而言，世界毀滅是必然而不能自主的最終結果，就如我們生活中的世界。

  • Press the "Transmigration" button and a new ecosystem starts, but this not the pervious one anymore.
    世界毀滅後按 "輪迴 (Transmigration)"按鈕便建構成新的世界，但已是另一個世界。

Artist Statement 創作自述:

The work "Mini Nature I"is a food chain close system with 4 types of creatures,basically is a virtual ecosystem. When 'outside factors' break into the system, the system will crash, but when no 'outside factors' break in, entropy/error cumulate, therefore the system will crash in the end.

'Mini Nature II – Live video Life"is a battleground between 2 races, Light Whorl and Dark Whorl. One is stronger in the light and the other is stronger in the dark. With changing the live picture through the cam, visitor may change whorls environment, therefore, directly affecting their life and dead. we can tread this as an open system, 'outside factor' is in charge of it. But in another way, this world and visitor tougher is a close system.

Compareing this 2 virtual world and the real world;the discussion of 'Open System vs.Close System' is actually the discussion of 'God Creation vs. Big Bang Theory'.

But no matter which side you are on, you have to believe there must be an end to our world.

作品"迷你生態"由4種生物組成一個食物鍊封閉系統，基本上是一個虛疑生態系統。當"外在因素"介入系統，系統會失衡，但再沒有"外在因素"介入下，系統仍然會因為熵/混亂的累積而失衡，換言之最終難逃一亡。

"迷理生態 II – 實況視頻生態"則是兩種敵對物種光螺和暗螺的戰場。它們一族善戰於光，另一族強大於暗，觀者透過操縱攝影機能直接操縱它們的生死。 我們可以把這個世界視為一個開放系統，"外在因素"在左右大局。 但另一方面看，這個世界和觀者加起來又是一個封閉系統。

把兩個虛擬世界和現實世界一起比較，開放系統和封閉系統的議題，其實是"神創論和自生論"議題的變奏。

但不管你相信那一套，你都必雖相信，世界最終難逃一亡。
