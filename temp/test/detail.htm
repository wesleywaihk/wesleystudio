Description At HK Gold Coast Residences you'll find one of the Hong Kong's largest outdoor free-form pools, landscaped gardens, tennis courts and many more leisure facilities. Residents can even book a private cruise on our 58-foot luxury yacht.

Within a few minutes walk, you will discover one of Hong Kong's longest beaches, Golden Beach, as well as Gold Coast Piazza, Gold Coast Yacht & Country Club and Hong Kong's leading five-star resort hotel – the Hong Kong Gold Coast Hotel. Transportation is also very convenient with frequent shuttle buses between Hong Kong Gold Coast and Central (30 minutes), TST (25 minutes), Tsuen Wan (15 minutes) and the airport (20 minutes). Shenzhen is also just 20 minutes away.
