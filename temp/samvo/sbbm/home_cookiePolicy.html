what are cookies?
statement

we automatically collect information about customer transactions and bets that are executed on our product and services (the website) via the use of cookies. a 'cookie' is a small piece of information sent by our web server to your web browser, which enables the server to collect information from your web browser

how we use of cookies
  1. allowing you to login to the website(s) and to check that your computer is still logged in to the same session while you browse from page to page.
  2. placing a bet, information will be temporarily stored in a cookie until the transaction has been completed. this enables you to navigate around the site making further selections without the need of losing your original slip and also enables us to provide you with a quick and effective service.
  3. we also use cookies to enable us to store your preferred account settings, for example: language, preferred odds display. this information is stored so that when you return you can benefit from your preselected settings and get the most of our products and services.
  4. we also use cookies to track customer browsing patterns on our products and services this enables us to tailor the service and/or products we offer you.
  5. cookies are used to build a demographic profile for a customised browser experience i.e. language, information pages etc…
  6. cookies are also used for your protection, we may use these for investigation purposes and identify any irregular activity from what you normally undertake
how can i turn off cookies?

you can block the tracking of your cookies by modifying your browser and privacy settings. blocking cookies may differ from web browser to web browser visit: www.aboutcookies.org for more information.

if you block the use of cookies, you may experience difficulties accessing our products and services. certain functionalities within the website may not function, for which we will not be responsible.
