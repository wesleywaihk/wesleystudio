• is there a complaint handling procedure?

    yes, we aim to resolve customers´ complaints with our first response and are determined to strictly follow the guidelines set by our licensing body: alderney gambling control commission.

    there are occasions where a customer may feel so aggrieved with a particular experience that they feel the need to speak to a senior manager or managing director directly. whilst we will always endeavor to consider these requests, we would ask our customers to contact their assigned betbroker and our customer service team in the first instance in order to give us the opportunity to deal with their complaint in a consistent manner.

  • when should i make a complaint?

    for any bet or trade that is in dispute, you should ideally contest this within 24 hours from the time the assigned betbroker reverts and confirms the bet with you. in terms of any disputes arising from your statement history these should ideally be raised within a 7-day period.

    all disputes should be raised to us by contacting us via email at cs@samvo.com or please give us a call at 08708 30 20 30.

  • how should i make the complaint?

    all disputes should be raised to us by contacting us via email at cs@samvo.com or please give us a call at 08708 30 20 30.

    all complaints will be recorded and reported following the guidelines of the samvo complaint handling policy as well as those set under our governing bodies.

  • what is the complaint handling procedure?

    samvo is determined to resolve customer complaints at first contact. however, we strongly believe that an effective escalation process will maximise our chances of resolving customer complaints effectively, professionally and efficiently. should you feel that your query has not been resolved to your satisfaction after you have spoken to a member of the customer service team, you can request for the matter to be escalated to a supervisor or a manager who will look into your query and provide you with a response within our response time. you will be given the name of the person who your query has been referred to and if applicable a time he/she will call you back.if you still feel that the matter is not solved up to your total satisfaction, we will arrange a higher level of management or director to speak with you in aiming to resolve your matter. we will let you know the name of the senior manager or director and respond to you as soon as we can. in cases whereby all available sources have been exhausted to resolve your complaint and you still find that you are not happy with the resolution it is within your rights to formally escalate your complaint to our regulators. you can email the alderney gambling control commission: info@agcc.gg
